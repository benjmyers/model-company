An uncommon look at the common Civil War soldier.

Charles Renard, far left, of Mess 2 poses with a friend from the 53rd Pennsylvania Infantry. Captain George A. Brooks, center, was killed in action at Antietam on Sept. 16th, 1862. Private Amos M. Wenrich, above, drowned Jan. 31st, 1862 while guarding Dam. No. 6 outside of Hancock, MD. SOURCE: Private collection.
Mess information was collected from six lists of missing equipment, created after the Third Battle of Winchester on May 25th, 1862. SOURCE: Private collection.
